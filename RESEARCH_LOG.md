## Hard-won lessons from prior in-house work (do NOT repeat these dead ends)
From `/apps/jepa-trader/`, `/apps/trading-system/`, `/apps/crypto-trader/`:
- Directional ML on price/TA features FAILS net of costs (equities daily & intraday; crypto). Confirmed across many model versions and three projects.
- Daily LLM news-sentiment as a directional signal: REJECTED (significant negative t-stats in equities).
- PEAD / earnings-drift FOLLOW: negative. RL: failed out-of-sample (Sharpe −5.33).
- Intraday / micro-horizon strategies die to transaction costs; micro-horizon optimization overtrades catastrophically.
- Crypto ML system lost money live (−$409 / −16–21% over 104 days). **67% of its "winning" models were degenerate constant predictors** that gamed a backtester which never checked prediction diversity. → MANDATORY: prediction-diversity / degenerate-signal filter before ranking any model.
- In-sample / insufficiently-purged Sharpe is worthless — every project shows backtest-to-live collapse. Trust only purged walk-forward, deflated Sharpe, PBO.
- The short side is where crypto money died (87% of losses) — be skeptical of systematic shorting.
- The only equity survivor: daily Bollinger mean-reversion (~+0.3 Sharpe). The only crypto survivors: simple rule-based daily swing strategies.

Deribit DVOL predicted forward realized vol at IC 0.67 vs 0.55 (trailing RV); VRP ≈ +7.7 vol points, IV>RV ~70% of periods, positive every year 2021–2026, carry Sharpe ~+1.07 net of a 2-vol-point cost. Caveat: harvesting needs options execution not yet modeled, and directional perp returns stayed efficient. **This is Paper #1.**

## Rigor protocol (ported to alpha_research/eval/rigor.py)
Every study must pass: purged/embargoed walk-forward CV; deflated Sharpe ratio; PBO via CSCV; non-overlapping t-stats (t>3 hurdle for a real claim); realistic transaction costs (full spread + fees + funding/slippage); prediction-diversity / degenerate-signal check; survivorship-free / point-in-time universes; benchmark every signal against the relevant trivial baseline AND the +0.3-Sharpe mean-reversion floor.

- **2026-06-16 (Paper 1, rigor pass — `scripts/02_vrp_rigor.py` + `alpha_research/factors/vrp.py`)** — Stress-tested the first pass. Results in `experiments/paper1_vrp_rigor.json`. The conclusion sharpens from "real but decayed" to **"real as a close-to-close variance-swap premium, but fragile and likely not harvestably-net."**
  - **CRITICAL — realized-vol-estimator fragility (a researcher degree-of-freedom that flips the result).** The premium and harvest Sharpe depend heavily on how realized vol is measured:
    - BTC: close-to-close **+8.90 vp** (t=4.45, Sh=1.53) → Parkinson **+4.59** (t=2.20, Sh=0.42) → Garman-Klass **+3.10** (t=1.40, Sh=−0.04).
    - ETH: c2c **+6.06** (t=2.14) → Parkinson **+0.63** (t=0.26) → GK **−1.24** (t=−0.23) — **ETH VRP vanishes/reverses under range-based RV.**
    Why it matters: a variance swap *settles* on close-to-close realized variance (so +8.9 is contractually correct for a swap), but a **delta-hedged option harvest** — the only realistic way to trade crypto vol on Deribit (no liquid variance swaps) — realizes the *path* variance, which range estimators (Parkinson/GK) proxy better. Range RV is higher → the harvestable premium roughly halves (BTC) or disappears (ETH). The c2c framing is the most generous; the harvest-relevant number is much thinner. (This is the methodology checklist's "non-standard errors" risk, live.)
  - **Phase robustness:** BTC Sharpe mean 1.18 (std 0.40, [0.46,1.96]); ETH 0.71 (std 0.26, [0.17,1.18]). Sign stable across all 60 entry phases; phase-0 was on the high side.
  - **Selection rigor (full-sample, c2c):** best config (gated, no vol-target, 0 cost) → **deflated Sharpe BTC 0.88, ETH 0.55; PBO = 0.00** over 60 configs. The full-sample premium is **NOT a selection/overfitting artifact** — it is statistically real. (DSR does not capture the separate, real regime-decay effect.)
  - **Decay reconfirmed:** BTC 2.82 (2021-23) → 0.27 (2024-26); ETH 1.94 → −0.13.
  - **Converging Paper-1 conclusion:** the crypto VRP **sign is real and not overfit** (DSR 0.88 BTC, PBO 0), but the **harvestable net-of-realistic-execution edge is thin-to-absent** — it halves/vanishes under harvest-relevant (range/path) RV, concentrates in 2021-2023, decays to ~0 (BTC)/negative (ETH) by 2024-2026, and true options-execution costs can't be modeled historically (snapshot-only surface). ETH is the weak case throughout. The honest "real premium, not a reliable net-of-cost alpha for us today" result. **Remaining for Paper 1:** equity cross-asset benchmark (running in parallel), HF (1h) RV robustness for 2024+, options-execution cost calibration from the snapshot + literature, figures, and the HTML write-up.
