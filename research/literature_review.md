# Literature Review — Synthesis & Research Program

_Generated by the `alpha-lit-review` workflow (25 agents, 12 web-grounded + adversarially-verified areas), 2026-06-16._

## Executive summary

Where alpha plausibly lives net of costs, given ALL the surveyed+verified evidence and our hard-won in-house ground truth: (1) THE VOLATILITY/VARIANCE RISK PREMIUM is the most defensible edge and is correctly the lead. Its SIGN is rock-solid (implied > realized ~70-88% of months across equities and crypto; jepa-trader already showed DVOL beats trailing RV at IC 0.67 vs 0.55, VRP ~+7.7 vol pts, positive every year 2021-2026). But the HARVESTABLE net-of-cost alpha is far thinner than headline Sharpes: in liquid US equity/VIX it is ~zero since ~2010 (Dew-Becker-Giglio: traded-option alpha indistinguishable from zero, converging to frictionless 'synthetic' options), and in crypto it is larger (~7x) but visibly DECAYING (DVOL ~70->45 in 2025-26, skew-gap to TradFi 15.3->0.2 pts) and brutally tail-exposed (BTC -53% into Feb-2026; the famous crypto 'short-vol Sharpe ~0.8' is a MISATTRIBUTION — it is spot-BTC buy-and-hold). Net read: only DEFINED-RISK, short-dated, vol-targeted, regime-CONDITIONED short-vol with explicit tail caps has a plausible modest net edge (~0.4-0.8), and passive option-selling LOSES money. (2) STRUCTURAL CRYPTO PREMIA harvest a financing/risk-transfer fee, not a price forecast, so they are the next-best bets but are crowding fast: delta-neutral funding/basis carry was spectacular gross (Sharpe 6.45 full-sample) but fell to 4.06 in 2024, went NEGATIVE in 2025, and the Oct-2025 cascade ($19B liquidated, USDe de-pegged ~35%) forcibly liquidated 'neutral' books — so it survives only diversified, regime-gated, sized for the de-peg, expecting high-single-to-mid-teens APY with gut-punch tails, NOT a smooth Sharpe-6. The cleanest cross-sectional crypto survivor is a short-horizon TREND factor (CTREND: breakeven 141bps >> realized costs, works in liquid coins), NOT size (microcap artifact) or market-neutral momentum (short legs liquidate). (3) MICROSTRUCTURE 'alpha' is real only as EXECUTION help: OFI/micro-price reduce slippage ~10-30% of half-spread (high confidence), but standalone sub-second LOB trading is a latency-rank race owned by ~6 firms, and passive market-making/AMM LP is 99.991% beta with an often-NEGATIVE residual. Short-term reversal reframed as liquidity provision (Nagel) is the honest, VIX/DVOL-conditional version and maps onto our prior +0.3-Sharpe mean-reversion survivor. WHERE ALPHA DOES NOT LIVE (consistent with our prior failures): directional ML on price/TA, daily LLM-sentiment, PEAD-follow, RL, naive size/value/BAB/momentum net of costs, individual-currency FX carry (~0 net), curve-curvature commodity strategies (net-negative), and any passive short-vol or maker book backtested without a tail/queue model. FX and commodity carry are the most-documented anomalies but have decayed to ~0.2-0.4 net (FX efficient-carry 1.08->0.25 post-GFC; commodity curve-slope 1.74->0.20) — worth running as cross-asset robustness checks, not as primary edges. The deepest meta-finding: with our trial budget and short crypto history, selection bias is the dominant risk (False Strategy Theorem: ~7 binary params => expected max Sharpe >2.6 from pure noise), so RIGOR IS THE EDGE — DSR/PBO/CPCV gating plus honest per-asset cost modeling will (correctly) reject most candidates, and that rejection is the deliverable. DATA REALITY CHECK: our Deribit DVOL+surfaces (2021-2026) and perp funding panel are confirmed and sufficient for Papers 1-2, BUT the in-house funding CSVs start ~Sept-2025 (timestamp 1758538800090) and the top-50 1h bars start ~Jan-2025 — i.e. our crypto window largely MISSES the 2024 carry boom and over-samples the post-ETF compression, which biases carry backtests DOWN (honest) but starves the decay test; and the option surfaces are a single end-of-sample SNAPSHOT (not a time series), so the variance-strip replication must be calibrated on the snapshot while the time-series harvest runs off DVOL + OHLCV realized vol.

## Paper plans

### Paper 1 (LEAD) — The Volatility Risk Premium, Cross-Asset: Is It Harvestable Net of Costs?
**Core question:** The SIGN of the VRP is settled (implied>realized). The real question: net of realistic options execution (fees + bid-ask + delta-hedge slippage + tail losses), is there a positive, decay-aware, defined-risk Sharpe in crypto (Deribit BTC/ETH) and/or equities (SPX/VIX) — and is the crypto premium genuine excess or just high vol-beta? This converts jepa-trader's one IC-level win (DVOL beats trailing RV) into an honest harvestable-PnL claim.

**Hypotheses:**
- H1 (sign, crypto): monthly DVOL^2 (risk-neutral 30d var) exceeds subsequent 30d realized var (Garman-Klass/Parkinson on 1h bars) in >65% of overlapping windows, mean gap +8 to +14 vol pts, 2021-2026. FALSIFIABLE if mean VRP<=0 or not Newey-West significant.
- H2 (harvest net): a vol-targeted, regime-gated, defined-risk short straddle / short risk-reversal on BTC/ETH (delta-hedged with perps, long OTM put wing) earns net Sharpe > the +0.3 mean-reversion floor AND > a passive always-on short-vol baseline. FALSIFIABLE if net Sharpe <= 0 after Deribit fees + OTM half-spread + hedge slippage, or if one in-sample jump erases cumulative PnL.
- H3 (decay): rolling 6-month net Sharpe of the crypto short-vol proxy declines across 2021->2026 (DVOL 70->45). FALSIFIABLE if Sharpe is stable rather than declining.
- H4 (term structure): 7-14d short-vol carries higher VRP-Sharpe than 30-90d (downward-sloping Sharpe, Dew-Becker). FALSIFIABLE if long-dated >= short-dated.
- H5 (skew): downside VRP > upside VRP in crypto (put-wing richer). FALSIFIABLE if upside >= downside.
- H6 (cross-asset benchmark): equity CBOE-PUT/BXM-style writing nets ~0 Sharpe post-2020 (Dew-Becker-Giglio), establishing how much of crypto VRP is genuine excess vs vol-beta. FALSIFIABLE if equity net alpha t>2.
- H7 (predictability sign-flip): crypto VRP predicts forward BTC/ETH returns with a NEGATIVE (or zero) coefficient, opposite equities. FALSIFIABLE if positive and significant.

**Method:** Time-series harvest runs off DVOL (12h bars, 3822 rows/coin) + OHLCV realized vol; the option SURFACE (single end-of-sample snapshot) calibrates a variance-strip / straddle replication and fee/spread model but cannot itself be a time series. Build VRP_t = DVOL_t^2 - RV_{t->t+30d}. Backtest delta-hedged short straddle and short risk-reversal via the existing cost-aware engine (full spread + 2*fee round-trip, segment-aware non-overlapping holds), with perp-funding-based hedge cost from the 228-perp panel and Deribit fee = 0.03% of underlying capped at 12.5% of premium. Regime gate = contango term-structure proxy + DVOL>trailing RV; vol-target the position. Run the full rigor gauntlet. Cross-asset: replicate VIX^2-RV and CBOE-PUT-style writing on sourced free VIX/PUT-index data.

**Baselines:** +0.3-Sharpe daily Bollinger mean-reversion floor (the only prior equity survivor), passive always-on short straddle (no gating, no vol-target) — must be BEATEN to prove the gating IS the edge, buy-and-hold spot BTC/ETH (the asset whose 0.8 Sharpe was misattributed as the short-vol number), trailing-RV-only volatility forecast (jepa's IC 0.55 baseline that DVOL's 0.67 beat), equity CBOE PUT/BXM index net returns as the 'VRP is ~dead net' control

**Data:** HAVE: Deribit DVOL_{BTC,ETH}.parquet + price_{BTC,ETH}.parquet (12h, 2021-03-24..2026-06-16) and surface_{BTC,ETH}.json (BTC 932 / ETH 762 contracts, mark_iv+strike+moneyness+cp+exp), HAVE: top-50 OHLCV 1h/4h/1d for realized variance (Garman-Klass/Parkinson), HAVE: 228-perp funding history for delta-hedge cost modeling, NEED (free): VIX/VVIX/SKEW + VIX term structure (Cboe, FRED VIXCLS) and CBOE PUT/BXM/WPUT index history for the equity benchmark

**Kill criteria:** Declare an honest negative if: (a) the gated, defined-risk, vol-targeted crypto short-vol net Sharpe is <= the +0.3 floor OR statistically indistinguishable from the passive baseline after full costs; OR (b) a single in-sample jump (e.g. a synthetic Oct-2025-style gap) erases cumulative PnL (ruin); OR (c) DSR < 0.95 at our true trial count or PBO >= 0.5; OR (d) net Sharpe decays monotonically to ~0 by 2025-2026 (premium arbitraged away in our live window). Any of these => publish 'the crypto VRP sign is real but not harvestable net-of-cost for us', which is itself a first-class result.

### Paper 2 — Crypto Carry: The Funding-Rate Cross-Section, Net of Costs and the Liquidation Tail
**Core question:** Is delta-neutral perpetual funding/basis carry a harvestable edge for us NET of taker fees, funding accrual, slippage, and — decisively — the liquidation/de-peg tail, given that the premium has demonstrably decayed (6.45->4.06->negative 2025) and our data window sits in the post-ETF compression?

**Hypotheses:**
- H1 (carry as signal): sorting perps by trailing 7-30d funding and going short-high/long-low-funding (delta-neutral) earns positive net carry. FALSIFIABLE if net Sharpe<=0 after 15bps round-trip + actual funding paid/received.
- H2 (decay within-window): mean funding and carry Sharpe compress monotonically across our 2025-2026 sample. FALSIFIABLE if stable/rising.
- H3 (carry predicts crashes): highest-funding (crowded-long) regimes cluster with Coinalyze liquidation spikes and negative forward skew (Brunnermeier-Pedersen / BIS +10%-carry->22%-OI-liquidated). FALSIFIABLE if no liquidation clustering.
- H4 (size = illiquidity, dies in top-50): restricting to top-50 liquid coins eliminates the size premium. FALSIFIABLE if a value-weighted size LS survives costs in top-50.
- H5 (funding as return predictor): extreme NEGATIVE funding predicts positive 1-7d spot returns (relief rally); extreme positive predicts elevated DVOL/crash. FALSIFIABLE if wrong-signed/insignificant.

**Method:** Build delta-neutral long-spot/short-perp (and cross-sectional long-low/short-high-funding) on the 228-perp panel + matched spot. Cost stack: taker ~4.5-5bps/leg + 8h funding accrual + slippage ~linear in (size/rolling-volume); model liquidation via Coinalyze OI/liquidation series and an explicit margin/de-peg stress (replay an Oct-2025-magnitude move). Value-weight, top-N liquid universe, point-in-time membership to kill survivorship. Full rigor gauntlet with special emphasis on CVaR/worst-day, not Sharpe (the payoff is short-vol-shaped).

**Baselines:** risk-free / T-bill rate (the trade must beat cash — by late-2025 the pure carry fell BELOW T-bills), buy-and-hold BTC, a naive flat-15bps-cost version vs the per-leg asymmetric-cost version (to show cost realism flips the conclusion), CTREND trend factor (Paper 2b sibling) as the alternative crypto edge

**Data:** HAVE: 228 perps x ~23k hrs funding (timestamp_ms,coin,funding_rate,premium); Coinalyze funding/OI/liquidations; top-50 OHLCV, NEED: spot prices matched to each perp's venue to compute true basis; per-venue maker/taker fee + funding-clamp parameters (free, exchange APIs); dead/delisted-coin history (CoinGecko/CMC snapshots) for survivorship

**Kill criteria:** Honest negative if: (a) diversified net carry Sharpe <= the +0.3 floor after per-leg costs + funding + a modeled de-peg/liquidation tail; OR (b) net carry falls below the risk-free rate in 2025-2026 (already the BIS/market finding); OR (c) the strategy's worst modeled drawdown (Oct-2025-style) exceeds ~1 year of accumulated carry (ruin); OR (d) DSR<0.95 / PBO>=0.5. Given our window misses the boom, a sub-floor result is likely and publishable as 'crypto carry was real but is crowded/decayed and tail-dominated.'

### Paper 3 — Statistical Arbitrage in Crypto: Cointegration / PCA Mean-Reversion, Honestly Out-of-Sample
**Core question:** Do crypto pairs/cointegration and PCA eigenportfolio (OU) stat-arb survive realistic costs in strict, no-look-ahead walk-forward — or are the headline ~100% in-sample win rates pure selection illusion (the literature's consistent finding)?

**Hypotheses:**
- H1: ADF-selected cointegrated pairs and PCA eigenportfolio OU mean-reversion on top-50 1h/4h bars net Sharpe <= 0.5 (often negative) OOS after 15bps + slippage, with cointegration vectors/eigenvectors re-estimated strictly out-of-sample. FALSIFIABLE if a walk-forward book nets Sharpe>1 after costs.
- H2: in-sample win rates >90% collapse OOS net of bid-ask bounce and spread reversals (only ~40% of >=20bps opportunities profitable after costs).
- H3: any apparent edge concentrates in low-liquidity coins (stale-price artifact) and vanishes in BTC/ETH/majors.

**Method:** Rolling estimation of cointegration (Engle-Granger/Johansen) and PCA factors with PURGED+EMBARGOED walk-forward (no pair/eigenvector chosen using test data). Trade residual OU reversion with cost-aware engine. Report PBO via CSCV across the pair/parameter universe (a multiple-testing minefield). Liquidity-tercile decomposition. Bid-ask-bounce guard.

**Baselines:** zero (does it beat not trading after costs?), +0.3 mean-reversion floor, a degenerate/constant-signal control (the prediction-diversity check is mandatory — 67% of a prior crypto system's 'winners' were constant predictors)

**Data:** HAVE: top-50 OHLCV 1h/4h/1d, NEED: crypto L2/order-book or at least per-coin spread series (derive from OHLCV high-low + volume, or free Binance depth/aggTrades) to make costs realistic; dead-coin history for survivorship

**Kill criteria:** Honest negative (the expected result) if OOS net Sharpe <= 0.5 / <= the floor, PBO >= 0.5, or the edge lives only in illiquid coins (stale-price mirage). No top-journal evidence of a robust net-positive crypto stat-arb book exists, so a clean negative is the likely, valuable contribution; an edge is claimed ONLY at t>3 + DSR>0.95 + PBO<0.5 after costs.

### Paper 4 — The Cost of Direction: An Honest Net-of-Cost Audit of the Directional Anomaly Zoo
**Core question:** Which classic cross-sectional anomalies (momentum, value, size, BAB/low-vol, short-term reversal, seasonality, PEAD, daily LLM-sentiment) survive REALISTIC costs on our 2024-2026 equities+crypto data — and how much is selection bias vs real?

**Hypotheses:**
- H1: most anomalies net <= 0 (consistent with prior in-house failures + McLean-Pontiff 26% OOS/58% post-pub decay); quality/profitability (low turnover) is the only survivor.
- H2: BAB/low-vol's apparent alpha is a rank-weighting/microcap artifact that ~halves under value-weighting and dies under a ~60% cost haircut.
- H3: net Sharpe is a steep negative function of turnover, with ~50%/month the survivor threshold (Novy-Marx-Velikov) — i.e. costs, not signal, decide.
- H4: crypto size/volume 'anomalies' are microcap artifacts that vanish in top-50; LLM-sentiment and PEAD-follow stay negative (re-confirming prior work).
- H5 (forking paths): enumerating >=100 defensible specs per anomaly, the in-sample t-distribution spans <2 and >3, and the median spec is far below the best — fragility, not edge.

**Method:** Standardized factor construction (value-weighted, NYSE-style/top-N-liquid breakpoints, point-in-time, winsorized) on S&P 500 (FnSpID fundamentals + bars) and top-50 crypto. Per-anomaly net-of-cost backtest + a specification-curve / non-standard-error multiverse + DSR with the project's true trial count + PBO. Apply the Harvey-Liu non-linear haircut (not flat 50%) and a t>3 hurdle.

**Baselines:** the +0.3 mean-reversion floor (the only prior equity survivor), Ken French / Open-Source-Asset-Pricing validated factor returns (free) to calibrate decay, zero net after costs

**Data:** HAVE: S&P 500 daily/15m/1h + FnSpID fundamentals + sentiment panel; top-50 crypto OHLCV, NEED (free): Ken French Data Library + openassetpricing.com (Chen-Zimmermann) for benchmark factor series and the replication toolkit (JKP/Chen open-source code)

**Kill criteria:** This paper EXPECTS mostly negatives — the kill criterion is inverted: an anomaly is CLAIMED as harvestable only if it clears t>3 + DSR>0.95 + PBO<0.5 + net-positive after per-asset costs + a tight (low non-standard-error) specification curve. Anything failing those is reported as an honest negative; the headline deliverable is 'how much of the zoo survives for a small, cost-realistic shop' (likely: only low-turnover quality).

### Paper 5 — Liquidity Provision as Alpha: Reversal, Order-Flow Imbalance, and the Fill-Model Illusion
**Core question:** Reframing our 56M SPY/QQQ LOB + 56GB Databento L2 NOT as directional HFT alpha (structurally unavailable) but as (a) execution-cost reduction via OFI/micro-price and (b) the liquidity-provision premium (vol-conditional reversal) — what survives a HONEST queue/adverse-selection fill model?

**Hypotheses:**
- H1 (fill-model illusion): a naive maker backtest assuming fills at the touch with no queue model shows positive PnL that FLIPS negative once a Moallemi-Yuan queue/adverse-selection fill model is added. FALSIFIABLE if passive-fill PnL minus 15s post-fill adverse selection stays > 0.
- H2 (OFI is contemporaneous, not predictive): integrated multi-level OFI explains ~80%+ contemporaneous mid variance but lagged predictive power is small and decays in <1 min — and a marketable-order strategy nets NEGATIVE after the spread.
- H3 (execution overlay works): using micro-price as an entry gate shaves 10-30% of the half-spread vs naive market orders (the real, harvestable use).
- H4 (reversal=LP): vol-conditional reversal (VIX/DVOL-scaled, large-cap/lower-volume) nets a small positive Sharpe whose conditional Sharpe rises with vol — the honest standalone.

**Method:** Build OFI (Cont-Kukanov-Stoikov), integrated multi-level OFI (PCA), and Stoikov micro-price from the LOB; Lee-Ready trade signing from ITCH for effective/realized-spread decomposition. Two matched backtests (naive-touch vs queue-model) to expose the illusion. Reversal sleeve via the cost-aware engine with VIX-conditioning. Square-root impact law (G~Y*sigma*sqrt(Q/V)) as the capacity/cost model.

**Baselines:** naive-touch maker backtest (the illusion) vs queue-model backtest (the truth), trailing-RV-only execution-timing vs OFI-gated execution, +0.3 mean-reversion floor for the reversal sleeve

**Data:** HAVE: 56M SPY/QQQ 10-level LOB snapshots (Nov-Dec 2025 + live); 56GB Databento Nasdaq L2 ITCH; S&P bars; free VIX, NEED: longer SPY/QQQ LOB history (accumulate live / source more ITCH months — current window too short for stable thresholds); crypto L2 if extending OFI to crypto

## Methodology checklist
- TRIAL ACCOUNTING IS NON-NEGOTIABLE: pre-register and persist N = every configuration evaluated per anomaly family (grid points, hyperparameter sweeps, feature sets, discarded variants). With our trial budget the False Strategy Theorem dominates (~7 binary params => expected max annualized Sharpe >2.6 from pure noise). If N is unknown the backtest is worthless. For correlated grids, estimate EFFECTIVE N via clustering of trial returns — naive raw N over-deflates.
- DEFLATED SHARPE RATIO >= 0.95 (alpha_research/eval/rigor.deflated_sharpe_ratio) using actual N, cross-trial Sharpe variance, sample length T, AND realized skew/kurtosis. Non-normality is decisive for short-vol/carry (negative skew + fat tails); never report a naive annualized Sharpe for a left-skewed payoff.
- PBO via CSCV < 0.5 (ideally near 0), S=16 -> 12,870 combinations (alpha_research/eval/probability_of_backtest_overfitting). A single hold-out or k-fold CANNOT detect overfitting — it assumes one trial.
- PURGED + EMBARGOED WALK-FORWARD / CPCV for any CV on overlapping-label or autocorrelated data: embargo = max label horizon; purge training windows overlapping the test set. Standard k-fold leaks future info and inflates OOS metrics. Pre-register the train/test split (e.g. crypto 2021-2023 train / 2024-2026 test).
- NON-OVERLAPPING t-STAT with |t| > 3 for any real claim (NOT t>2; Harvey-Liu-Zhu, and the project explicitly raised the bar). Thin by the label horizon; use Newey-West/Hodrick for overlapping-window regressions. Be aware full forking-path honesty pushes the bar even higher (Coqueret t>=8.2), so report a specification curve, not a single best path.
- PREDICTION-DIVERSITY / DEGENERATE-SIGNAL CHECK — MANDATORY before any model is ranked. A prior in-house crypto system lost money because 67% of its 'winning' models were constant predictors gaming a backtester that never checked diversity. Verify every signal is not a disguised constant, a single-factor proxy, or pure market/vol-beta (use CAPM/factor-alpha, RankMe effective rank, prediction dispersion).
- REALISTIC, PER-ASSET, ASYMMETRIC TRANSACTION COSTS: full bid-ask half-spread crossed each side + per-side fees + funding accrual + slippage (square-root impact G~Y*sigma*sqrt(Q/V)). Crypto: Deribit 0.03%-of-underlying/12.5%-cap on options, ~5bps perp taker + funding; option OTM spreads several % of premium; small-coin spreads 50-100bps+. Report gross AND net and the gross-to-net collapse. A single flat-bps assumption is a known false-alpha source (flip-the-conclusion test: per-leg cost vs flat cost).
- TAIL-AWARE METRICS, NOT SHARPE ALONE, for any short-vol / carry / liquidity-provision strategy: report CVaR(1%), worst single day, MaxDD, Sortino, and an explicit STRESS/synthetic-jump scenario (Volmageddon / Mar-2020 / LUNA / FTX / Oct-2025 magnitude). Backtests that don't SPAN a tail event massively overstate Sharpe (peso problem).
- SURVIVORSHIP-FREE / POINT-IN-TIME UNIVERSES: reconstruct historical top-N membership and include delisted/dead coins and stocks. Microcap/illiquid contamination (microcaps = 60.7% of equity names, 3.2% of cap; small alts in crypto) manufactures fake premia — value-weight and screen by liquidity (top-N liquid), and treat illiquid-asset reversal/short-term signals as stale-price artifacts until proven otherwise.
- BENCHMARK EVERY SIGNAL against (a) its trivial baseline (e.g. trailing-RV vol forecast, buy-and-hold, the passive always-on version), (b) the +0.3-Sharpe daily Bollinger mean-reversion FLOOR (the only prior equity survivor — the practical 'is this even worth it' bar), and (c) for multi-factor work, AQR QSPIX's ~0.41 live net Sharpe as the realistic professional ceiling. An edge must beat the floor net of costs AND clear DSR/PBO/t>3 to be claimed; otherwise it is reported as an honest negative.
- NON-STANDARD-ERRORS / SPECIFICATION-CURVE discipline: defensible researcher choices (window, weighting, breakpoint, winsorization, rebalance freq, universe filter) generate dispersion on par with statistical error (Menkveld 2024). A result that lives or dies on one arbitrary choice is fragile — plot the multiverse and report the dispersion, especially for crypto where non-standard errors EXCEED equities.
- DECAY / REGIME-STABILITY TEST: report rolling and pre/post-break sub-period Sharpes (post-2008, post-financialization, post-ETF for crypto, by-year). Anomalies decay ~26% OOS / ~58% post-publication; crypto carry went 6.45->4.06->negative. A static backtest over a favorable regime (e.g. 2020-2021 crypto bull) is fiction — weight recent sub-periods and watch for live decay.
